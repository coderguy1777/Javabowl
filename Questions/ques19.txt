The perched variety of these structures usually sit above their confined type. These structures are
usually found in the phreatic zone below the vadose zone. The reduction of these structures volume is a
major cause of human induced subsidence. Much of the Keystone Pipeline controversy has revolved around
the gigantic Ogallala one of these structures. Overuse of these structures can lead to saline intrusion. When
these structures are under pressure, their drainage wells are called artesian. For 10 points, name these layers of
porous rock that are filled up to the water table.
