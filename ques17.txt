The second title character is given this trait in the most famous poem
from the manuscript Cotton Nero A.x which also includes the poems Patience and Purity. A character named
for this trait is described by Brom Bones near Tarrytown in a story written by Diedrich Knickerbocker
and included in The Sketch Book of Geoffrey Crayon. After proposing to Katrina Van Tassel Ichabod Crane sees a
Hessian trooper who was put in this state by a stray cannonball. This state is averted by Lady Bertilaks girdle after
Gawain puts the Green Knight in this state. For 10 points name this bodily state of a ghostly horseman in The
Legend of Sleepy Hollow.
